Each notebook runs the full pipeline for one region: data cleaning, feature engineering,
model training and comparison, evaluation, and forecasting.

## Objective 1 — Revenue Forecasting (Completed)

- **Regions:** Bahrain (AFS, AUB), Oman (ABO, SOH), Egypt, UAE
- **Pipeline (per notebook):**
  - Data cleaning — duplicate-date resolution, outage detection, missing-day imputation
  - Feature engineering — lag and rolling-window features, day-of-week and holiday flags,
    and Hijri-calendar indicators (Ramadan, Eid) to capture Islamic-calendar seasonality
  - Modelling — Decision Tree (baseline), Random Forest, Gradient Boosting, XGBoost
  - Evaluation — MAPE, MAE, RMSE
  - Forecasting — recursive daily forecast with visualisation
- **Result:** MAPE of 6–11% across regions
